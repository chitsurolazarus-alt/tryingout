Luu Travels is a private shuttle, 24/7, door-to-door, airport transfer and goods transportation provider, operating since 2023 across major cities including Johannesburg, Polokwane, and Durban. They offer secure, reliable, and modern transportation for individuals and groups (up to 60 people), featuring monitored vehicles that are rotated every service.
